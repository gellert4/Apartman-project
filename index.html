Rózsa Apartman

"Rózsa Apartman, a family-run retreat for over 90 years, is located just 50 meters from the scenic shores of Lake Balaton. Offering a perfect blend of relaxation and luxury, guests can enjoy two saunas, two outdoor pools, and a serene garden designed for peaceful escapes. The property also features a children's playground, making it ideal for families, while its luxurious and meticulously clean rooms ensure a comfortable stay. With ample parking space, Rózsa Apartman provides everything you need for a worry-free getaway, blending modern amenities with timeless hospitality."
